CYBER ADDICTION

Cyber addiction, also known as Internet addiction, computer addiction or online addiction refers to excessive or compulsive use of Internet and computers leading to impairment or distress (Shaw et.al. 2008). Children and adolescents who suffer from anxiety and depression are at greater risk of cyber addiction since they may use Internet to cope with their problems. Those with other addiction problems such as alcohol and drugs are also at greater risk. Adolescents who lack social support or not socially active tend to use Internet as their social networking sites, and gradually become addicted to it.
